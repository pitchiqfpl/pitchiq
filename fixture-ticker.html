How difficulty ratings are calculated: Overall uses the official FPL difficulty rating. Attacking uses opponent xGC per game (how leaky their defence is) — weighted 60% season average + 40% last-5 form, with a home/away multiplier. Defensive uses opponent xG per game (how dangerous their attack is) — same weighting. All scores are min-max normalised across all 20 teams then bucketed 1–5. xG from API-Football, updated after every matchday.
